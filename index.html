Quantum Numbers

n

Principal

Determines the energy level and overall size of the orbital. Higher n means the electron is further from the nucleus, with more energy.

l

Angular Momentum

Defines the shape of the orbital. l=0 is spherical (s), l=1 is dumbbell (p), l=2 is cloverleaf (d), l=3 is complex (f).

m

Magnetic

Specifies the orientation of the orbital in space. For a given l, m ranges from -l to +l, giving 2l+1 possible orientations.
